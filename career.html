Junior Quant/Traderv

Full Time

Lead

View Job

As a Junior Quant/TraderTrader, we’ll teach you how to use our proprietary technology while implementing sophisticated hedging and market making techniques, so you can make an impact from the bottom line of the firm, providing vital support to our senior traders and our customers. Your expertise in quantitative analysis and data analytics will play a vital role in identifying, tracking and maintaining financial metrics, both on-chain and off-chain, that will optimize hedging decisions on a daily basis.

You will also take part in developing new, innovative financial products, interact with our in-house software developers as well as support daily execution and settlement operations.

This is a unique opportunity for the right ambitious candidate to be a key early team member of a high growth, high quality start-up in a cutting edge-disruptive industry.

Key responsibilities

  • Develop & implement solutions for the real-time monitoring of equity and digital asset markets that create alpha for the company.
  • Contribute to embedding these strategies across the trading team.
  • Work alongside the Head of Trading to design strategies and analyse trends and opportunities, i.e idea generation, data gathering and research/analysis, model implementation and backtesting, in both ETP and cryptocurrency markets.
  • Support the product development team with relevant market metrics to inform the design of future product launches.
  • Support the trading team to ensure traded risk is properly reflected in positions and cash management scenario analyses.
  • Support the trading team in the clearing- and settlement process.

Qualifications

  • Degree in a quantitative field such as engineering, computer science, physics, mathematics or finance
  • Relevant work experience of 4-5 years in financial markets
  • Strong analytical skills and expertise in financial systems and reporting.
  • Proven ability in developing trading strategies, contributing to the structuring of innovative financial products and in project management.
  • Enthusiasm for empirical studies.
  • High computer literacy, programming knowledge a benefit.
  • Significant interest in asset markets in general, current trends and evolutions in the fintech industry, and cryptocurrencies in particular.
  • Excellent communication skills in English, other languages are a plus.

And you can:

  • Work independently, take initiative and be proactive
  • Run multiple projects simultaneously.
  • Be collaborative and strategic, interacting with diverse stakeholders across the business including; Product, Tech and Operations.
  • Maintain a growth mindset and ownership mentality.
